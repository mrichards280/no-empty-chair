The Visit

What to expect

A first visit should feel easy. Here's how yours will go.

i

Before you come

Book online in a minute, then fill out a short health & skin intake so we can tailor your treatment. Arrive 15 minutes early to change and settle in.

ii

While you're here

Phones rest in your locker. We keep the room quiet, the pressure to your liking, and the upselling at zero. This hour is yours.

iii

After

Water, a moment on the relaxation bench, and simple aftercare notes. Rebook at the desk for member pricing on your next visit.

Good to know. Reserving requires a card on file — no charge unless there's a late cancel.
